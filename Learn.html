OUR MISSION

Cornerstone is a non-profit organisation and we provide help to the community by offering alternative payment options to those in need. We offer donations in the form of subscription boxes of fresh fruit, vegetables, and cupboard goods from sustainable sources. To those who aren't comfortable in receiving donations, we offer alternative payment options such as volunteer work in our restaurant and warehouse.

MAYBE YOU'VE SEEN US AROUND?

Not interested in food but want something tangible for yourself? Cornerstone has a shop on location for the discerning customer with fantastic taste!

WHERE CAN I STAY UP TO DATE?

Want to stay updated on our menu or any charity work we are doing? We post content on social media on a variety of platforms. Whether it's for some food waste tips or events near you, come give us a follow and join the Cornerstone community!
