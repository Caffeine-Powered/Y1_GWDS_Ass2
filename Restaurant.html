Yes! We Are Tangible!

All the food that we take from our sources can be served to you in person.

Fluid & Flexible

As times and seasons change, the foods we source from farmers markets and supermarkets change. This means what's on our menu can be subject to change, but don't worry! We have a number of staple choices that we can regularly source that aren't going away. The rest of our menu will ebb and flow with what we can source, so if you see something you don't like, then rest assured that we will have an extensive range of alternatives available at any time.

On A Plate Or In A Box

Much of what you can find on our menu is available in our subscription boxes, which are options for those struggling financially. If you enjoy our food and are feeling a pinch then rest assured that we offer help at the same quality as what we serve to our customers
