Seasons Change, Brighter Skies!

Spring draws to a close already, how time flies! Summer crops such as peppers and berries are going to become more prominent in our boxes and restaurant. You can look forward to this and more soon!

Variety

We source our produce and products from local groceries and farmers markets. This means that what foods we have in our boxes and on our menu can vary from day to day and from box to box. Seasonal goods are also subject to change with different crops and foods coming in and out of season. Whilst the specifics of what you might get may change periodically, the one thing we can promise is that each meal in our restaurant and every item in your box will be fresh.

Help Is Always Available

Times can be tough, but we provide options for those who may be struggling financially. Student or family, young or old, we offer a subscription box to ensure that you can get fresh fruit, veg, and other cupboard necessities in exchange for volenteer work.

Optional Volenteer Work

If you don't feel comfortable receiving our food box as a donation, we offer volenteer work as an alternative payment method. In exchange for our subscription box, we take volenteers in our kitchen, working the floor, and in our warehouse. Volenteers will be working alongside our employees.

Where To Collect

Our subscription boxes can be collected from our warehouse, right next door to our restaurant!

Everything In Our Boxes Is In Our Restaurant Too!

Cornerstone are a non-profit organisation and we provide help to the community by offering donations and alternative payment options to those in need.
